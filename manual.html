Manual

Welcome to the Smart Watering System Manual Thank you for choosing our automatic plant watering machine — the effortless way to keep your plants healthy and thriving. This guide will help you set up, operate, and maintain your system so your plants get the perfect amount of water every time. Let’s get started on making plant care simple and worry-free.

When you start up the S.W.A.G., you will see the home screen. On this screen, you can find information about the temperature (in Celsius), humidity (in percentage), and light intensity (in lumens). It will also indicate when you need to refill the water compartment. To navigate to the settings screen, press the middle button.

When you enter the settings screen, you can navigate using the right button to move down and the left button to move up. To select a setting, press the middle button. To change a setting, use the left and right buttons. Once you’ve chosen the desired value, press the middle button again to confirm your selection. To return to the home screen, select Back. When watering is set to Auto, the system will water the plant automatically whenever the soil becomes too dry. When watering is set to Manual, you can specify a time interval for when the plant should be watered.
