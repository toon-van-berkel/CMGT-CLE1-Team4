We Are SWAG

Smart Watering Automatic Gadget — the effortless way to help your plants thrive. At SWAG, we believe taking care of plants should be easy, reliable, and stress-free. That’s why we created a smart watering system that ensures your plants stay healthy and fresh every single day. No more guessing, no more dry soil, and no more overwatering. Just perfectly balanced care — automatically. Our system uses advanced sensors to monitor each plant's individual needs. When the soil gets dry, SWAG knows exactly how much water to give — no more, no less. Whether you’re at home, on vacation, or busy with life, your plants are always in good hands.
